Our Story & Mission

TechNvst PTY Ltd was registered in 2017 in South Africa and is a strategic innovation commercialisation firm dedicated to scaling businesses through corporate collaboration and facilitating access to market and investment across Africa.

By combining international best practice with local expertise, TechNvst understands how to unearth new innovation while meeting the challenges faced by businesses today.

TechNvst targets high potential innovators and helps them to scale with a strong focus on ensuring meaningful engagements. TechNvst's unique approach to strategic market acquisition and tech enablement in collaboration with its associate partners has resulted in leading success rates and long-standing partnerships with top blue chip enterprises and other multinationals operating in Africa.
