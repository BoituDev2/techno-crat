Eligibility Criteria

Stage 2: Investor Offer

  • Affiliated with Microsoft for Startups Investor Network
  • Software-based product owned by the startup
  • Less than $350,000 in lifetime free Azure credits
  • Headquartered in an Azure-available country
  • Hasn't raised Series C & privately held
  • For-profit business (not consultancy, agency, crypto mining)

Stage 3: Pegasus Offer

  • Venture-backed B2B tech company with traction
  • Sectors: Retail, Healthcare, Cybersecurity, Data & AI
  • Solution transactable on Azure Marketplace
  • GTM Team in place (CRO & CMO)
  • Company less than 6 years old, $1M+ ARR
  • Signed up for Microsoft for Startups Portal
